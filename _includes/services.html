Cyber Common Operating Picture

Clarity precedes informed decisions.

Clarity facilitates informed decisions in the cyber space. This clarity depends on an keen awareness of cyber status of the organization, which has been distilled to the right fidelity level, and shared among stakeholders in an organization. The stated shared awareness of cyber status can be defined as a Cyber Common Operating Picture (CCOP). At a glance, CCOP can be understood as a compilation of multiple information from both within and without organization, distilled to the essence, and depicted selectively to stakeholders.

Demonstration

Detailed

Provide hierarchical, evidence-based metrics that reveal both high-level insights and details.

Customizable

Extensible and stable platform architecture that allows CCOP to adapt to organizations.

Smart

Generate more insights with data analytics and artificial intelligence.
